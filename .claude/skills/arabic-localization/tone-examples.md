## Quick Decision Matrix

**Choose Najdi if:**
- Target audience is Gulf region (KSA, UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain)
- Consumer-facing app (B2C)
- Casual, friendly brand voice
- Social features or community-driven
- Marketing and engagement priority

**Choose Professional if:**
- B2B SaaS platform
- Corporate/enterprise users
- Professional services
- Need balance between formal and accessible
- Multi-generation user base

**Choose MSA if:**
- Pan-Arab audience (Morocco to Iraq)
- Government or educational sector
- Legal/compliance requirements
- Formal documentation
- When in doubt - safest default
